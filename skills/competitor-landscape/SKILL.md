# Competitor Landscape

Use when `market-research` summary matrix is not enough — full positioning → `positioning-wedge` skill.

## Checklist

- [ ] List direct, indirect, and substitute solutions
- [ ] Capture pricing, audience, and core promise for each competitor
- [ ] Mine reviews and forums for recurring complaints
- [ ] Map whitespace and positioning options
- [ ] Optional: one-page **battle card** per top 2–3 competitors
- [ ] Record competitive kill criteria

## Interpretation

- Competition usually validates demand.
- No competitors can mean an unserved niche or a market that will not pay.
- Kill when a dominant incumbent blocks entry or the market is hyper-competitive without a wedge.
